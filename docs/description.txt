## Research Questions

1. How has the average cinema ticket price evolved over the years across different German states?
2. Which federal states have seen the highest and lowest number of cinema visits per inhabitant over time?
3. Is there a relationship between average ticket prices and the number of cinema visits per inhabitant in each state?
4. How does the number of cinemas and screens in a region relate to the number of cinema visitors?
5. How is the number of residents in a region related to the number of cinema visitors?

## Suitable Analysis Methods

1. Line Plot:
   - To show the development of average ticket prices over time per state.
   - X-axis: Year, Y-axis: Average ticket price (EUR), Lines: Different states.

2. Bar Plot / Line Plot / Heatmap:
   - To compare cinema visits per inhabitant across states and years.
   - Grouped bar plots or a heatmap can highlight high/low attendance patterns.

3. Scatter Plot:
   - To explore the relationship between ticket prices and cinema visits per inhabitant.
   - X-axis: Average ticket price, Y-axis: Cinema visits per inhabitant.

## Python Packages / Functions

- `pandas`: For data cleaning and processing.
- `matplotlib.pyplot`: For visualizations (lineplot, barplot, scatterplot).
- `scipy.stats.pearsonr`: To calculate correlation between ticket price and attendance.
- `numpy`: For numeric operations and manual correlation if needed.
